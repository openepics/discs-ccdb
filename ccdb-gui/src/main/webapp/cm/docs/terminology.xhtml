Term Definition Examples Device A device can be independently installed and de-installed as a single unit. It must be measured, tested, tracked, and serialized. It is generally the final product from an engineering group. Devices may be controlled by the control system. A device may contain other devices. A magnet or RF amplifier Slot A slot is a uniquely identifiable space. A device, subsystem, or system is hosted (installed, placed, stored etc) in a slot. A slot may contain other slots. A slot is identified by a name. FE_LEBT:FE_D1001, LS1_CA01, FE_MEBT, LS1 Slot Group A set of slots.
