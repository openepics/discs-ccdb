Term Definition Examples Assembly A collection of parts. A coldmass Device A device can be independently installed and de-installed as a single unit. It must be measured, tested, tracked, and serialized. It is generally the final product from an engineering group. Devices may be controlled by the control system. A device may contain other devices. A magnet or RF amplifier FRIB Name An identifier that adheres to the FRIB Naming Convention. It identifies a system, subsystem, slot, element, or signal. FE:CHLR_N0002, FE_LEBT:FC_D0123:I_RD Installation The process of placing a device in an appropriate slot and interfacing it with other devices. Placing a magnet in its slot in the tunnel. Nick Name A unique alias for a Part Number. It is a more meaningful name assigned by a department or a group. CAV85-ROARK Part A physical entity that forms the basic building block, for a domain. The term part is used to refer to both its design and instances. Coupler, Bellow, Flange Part Number An identifier that uniquely identifies design (or type) of a part or assembly. T30602-MDE-0140-0060 Note the part number does not have the drawing revision number Serial Number An identifier that uniquely identifies instance of a part or assembly. Part Number and Serial Number together uniquely identify an entity (assembly, device, or serialized part). S01234 Slot A slot is a uniquely identifiable space. It is identified by a name. A device, subsystem, or system is hosted (installed, placed, stored etc) in a slot. A slot may contain other slots. FE_LEBT:FE_D1001, LS1_CA01, FE_MEBT, LS1 Subsystem A set of one or more devices that together implement a higher functionality. A cryomodule, Bending Section, Beam Transport Section. System A set of subsystems that together implement a higher functionality. Linac segment, RF, Vacuum
